Our Commitment to Gender Equality

At Rooh, we proudly support UN Sustainable Development Goal 5: Gender Equality. Our commitment is to foster a beauty industry that celebrates and empowers every gender identity, inside and outside our company. We pledge to:

  • Maintain an inclusive and diverse workplace where every identity is respected.
  • Run awareness campaigns breaking beauty stereotypes.
  • Support equal opportunities and leadership development for all genders.
  • Use inclusive language in all communications and product descriptions.
  • Partner with organizations advocating for gender equality.
